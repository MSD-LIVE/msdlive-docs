## Meta-Repository Webinar
MSD Community of Practice webinar on using meta-repositories to document the data and code underpinning MSD experiments. The webinar covers the role of open science in the MSD community, the origins and purpose of meta-repositories, and step-by-step instructions and best practices for building a meta-repository.
